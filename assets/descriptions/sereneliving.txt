A good location for a stress-free getaway in Pune, Serene Living is a homestay surrounded by views of the quiet street. The property features a terrace, shared lounge, and parking on-site among other facilities. The property is around 4.4 miles from Darshan Museum, 4.5 miles from Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum, and 4.8 miles from Pune Junction Train Station. The homestay features inner courtyard views, a picnic area, a 24-hour front desk, and free Wifi is available throughout the property.

The units provide views of the garden and come with a seating area, a washing machine, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, and a private bathroom with slippers and free toiletries. At the homestay, each unit has bed linen and towels.
